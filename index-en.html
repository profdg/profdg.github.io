Who am I

My name is Darryl and I’m a South African living in Belo Horizonte, Brazil.

Why am I in Brazil (you may be asking yourself…) Because I met a beautiful Brazilian in South Africa and I just had to be where she was. Today, we are married!

Once I graduated from university and before moving to Brazil, I worked as an Account Manager and then as a Product Manager (Marketing) at Sony South Africa.

After packing my life into a suitcase and moving to Belo Horizonte, at the end of 2012, I discovered a new country and a new profession: that of an English teacher!

One of my greatest joys is being able to help people the best way I can, especially when I have the knowledge and tools for it. This is the methodology I bring to my life and to my classes. I really enjoy taking something that looks complicated, like English grammar, and turning it into a simple concept that anyone can understand and from which everyone can benefit.

This is my story... I’m look forward to meeting you!
