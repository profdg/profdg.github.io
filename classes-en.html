English Lessons

Methodology

My methodology is completely adapted to the necessities of the student. In case you wish to focus on conversation, grammar or learn English completely from A-Z, I adapt all classes to your specific needs.

Method and materials used:

  • Natural Approach®
  • Conversation classes and fluency development
  • Grammar focused learning
  • Games and online activities
  • Development of writing skills

Books used (among others):

  • Natural Approach - Cambridge Interchange 4th Edition
  • New English File - Iniciante - Avançado (Beginner - Advanced)
  • IELTS - Barron's IELTS Superpack
  • TOEFL - Barron's TOEFL Superpack

What is the 'Natural Approach'?

The 'Natural Approach' is great for beginners as the classes are completely in English from Day 1, and we use Portuguese only when absolutely necassary to explain difficult concepts that the student is struggling with. This format assists the student to 'think' in English and avoids them trying to translate word for word in their minds. The results of my students who have used this method have always been great.

How do the classes work?

Classes are held at a location of the students choosing (home, work, public place etc). If the student would like to study in a small group, they are free to create one.

The classes follow a rhythm that the student is comfortable with, and is constantly adapted and evolved meet to their specific needs.
